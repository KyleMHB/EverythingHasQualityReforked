[h1]Description of Mod[/h1]
[i]A fork of a fork that brings quality to far more things in RimWorld, with configurable categories, individual item control, search, and better settings performance for large modpacks.[/i]

Everything Has Quality Reforked expands RimWorld's quality system so more items can have quality while letting you decide exactly where quality applies.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*][b]Expanded quality support[/b] for buildings, resources, ingredients, meals, drugs, medicine, apparel, weapons, shells, and other manufactured items.
[*][b]Category-wide controls[/b] for quickly enabling or disabling quality by item group.
[*][b]Individual item overrides[/b] for resources, buildings, weapons, apparel, and other items.
[*][b]Searchable settings lists[/b] for large modpacks with many defs.
[*][b]Minimum and maximum quality limits[/b] for each supported category.
[*][b]Material, work-table, skill, and inspiration options[/b] for production quality logic.
[*][b]RimWorld 1.6 support[/b] with updated load folders and metadata.
[/list]

[h1]How to Use[/h1]
Enable the mod, then open RimWorld's mod settings and select [b]Everything Has Quality[/b].

Use the settings tabs to configure quality by category, adjust quality ranges, enable individual def customization, search long item lists, and apply category changes.

This mod enables quality on more things, but it does [b]not[/b] make every quality-bearing item automatically scale every stat. For broader stat effects, use a compatible companion mod such as [b]Quality Expanded[/b].

[h1]Compatibilities[/h1]
[list]
[*][b]Supported RimWorld version:[/b] 1.6.
[*][b]Required mod:[/b] Harmony.
[*][b]Load order:[/b] Load Harmony before this mod.
[*][b]Large modpacks:[/b] Searchable and cached settings lists are included to make long def lists easier to manage.
[/list]

No specific hard conflicts are documented in this repository.

[h1]Settings / Setup Instructions[/h1]
Settings are available through RimWorld's normal mod options menu under [b]Everything Has Quality[/b].

You can configure:
[list]
[*]Quality categories for buildings, resources, ingredients, meals, drugs, medicine, apparel, weapons, shells, and manufactured items.
[*]Minimum and maximum quality bounds per category.
[*]Whether materials, work tables, and skill requirements affect generated quality.
[*]Supply quality multipliers for awful through legendary inputs.
[*]Custom production inspirations for butchering, chemistry, construction, cooking, gathering, harvesting, mining, and stonecutting.
[*]Individual def inclusion lists for supported categories.
[/list]
